Safety Features

Our YOLOv11-based system delivers comprehensive hazard detection and real-time monitoring for modern mining operations.

PPE Compliance Detection

Automatically detects helmets, vests, goggles, gloves, and safety boots in real-time with 99.2% accuracy across all lighting conditions.

Fire & Smoke Detection

Early detection of fire, smoke, and sparks before they escalate. YOLOv11 identifies thermal anomalies and combustion signatures instantly.

Fall & Collapse Detection

Monitors structural integrity, detects rock falls, tunnel collapses, and unstable terrain. Alerts workers before disasters strike.

Process

How SafeMine AI Works

A seamless pipeline from camera feed to actionable safety alerts in milliseconds.

01

Camera Feed Acquisition

Industrial-grade cameras and CCTV feeds are deployed across mining sites, capturing real-time video streams from tunnels, open pits, processing plants, and worker zones.

02

YOLOv11 Inference Engine

Each frame is processed through our optimized YOLOv11 model running on edge GPUs. The model detects hazards, PPE compliance, equipment, and personnel simultaneously.

03

Threat Classification & Scoring

Detected objects are classified by severity — low, medium, critical. Confidence scores and spatial analysis determine the urgency of each alert.

04

Instant Alert & Response

Critical alerts are pushed to safety officers, site managers, and workers via sirens, mobile notifications, and dashboard overlays — all within 50 milliseconds.
